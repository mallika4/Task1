Debute

Pawan Kalyan is one of the most popular actors in the Telugu film industry and a well-known political leader in Andhra Pradesh. His real name is Konidela Kalyan Babu. He was born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla. He is the younger brother of famous actor Chiranjeevi. Because of his powerful acting style and strong personality, fans lovingly call him “Power Star.” Apart from acting, he is also the founder of the political party Jana Sena Party and actively participates in social and political activities.Pawan Kalyan entered the Telugu film industry in 1996 with the movie Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi. This movie introduced him as a new actor, and audiences noticed his unique style and martial arts skills. Although the movie had moderate success, it helped him gain recognition and opened the door for more opportunities in the Telugu film industry

political carrer

In 2014,Pawan Kalyan entered politics and started his own party called Jana Sena Party. His aim was to work for the welfare of people and bring positive change to society. He is known for speaking strongly about public issues such as youth development, farmers’ problems, and social justice.On 12 June 2024, Pawan Kalyan was sworn in as a cabinet minister in the Government of Andhra Pradesh, and on 14 June 2024, he was announced as the deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh.[175] He is also the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology
